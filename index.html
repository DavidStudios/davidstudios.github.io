What is DSans?


DSans is a font designed for websites. It's a beautiful font meant to present a welcoming & comfortable experience for users.



Why use DSans?


DSans is more soft than other fonts, and isn't jagged, so users feels your page is clean and sleek.

How do I get DSans

Getting DSans is very simple. Just paste this code into your CSS style:
@import url('https://dsans.davidstudios.uk/css2.css');

When that is done, just define either "DSans Regular" or "DSans Bold" in your font-family.
